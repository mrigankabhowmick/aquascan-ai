Our Idea 💡

AquaScan AI: A Novel Approach

Our proposed solution is a low-cost, AI-powered optical sensor for real-time microplastic detection in water.

How It Works

Combines an AI model with a custom low-cost optical sensor to analyze water samples in real-time, with results available on a mobile app and cloud dashboard.

Unique Selling Points

Up to 10x cheaper than existing solutions. Provides an open data platform for global collaboration. Portable, pocket-sized design powered by TinyML for on-device inference.

Feasibility & Viability 📈

Can It Be Done? Yes.

Our solution is designed to be practical, affordable, and scalable.

Low Cost

Device cost under ₹3000, making it accessible for wide-scale deployment.

Solar Powered

Designed for off-grid operation with an optional solar power module.

Pocket-Sized

Highly portable for in-field analysis by anyone, anywhere.

Challenges

Water turbidity, potential for false positives, and limited public datasets for training.

Solutions

Controlled illumination to handle turbidity, generating synthetic data to reduce false positives, transfer learning, and over-the-air (OTA) model updates.
